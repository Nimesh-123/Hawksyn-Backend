Star label system
1★ = Poor · 2★ = Fair · 3★ = Good · 4★ = Really good · 5★ = Excellent ✦. The ✦ spark on 5-star makes a perfect score feel special — small delight. Labels appear instantly on tap, amber colour.
Unrated = dimmed
Third category not yet rated — card is at 60% opacity, "Tap to rate" hint below the stars. Not blocking. User can submit with just 2 categories rated. All 3 optional — reduces friction.
