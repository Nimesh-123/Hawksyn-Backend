J5-S01b
Profile pic — source picker sheet (camera vs gallery)
Picker open
Bottom sheet
Tapping "Add a photo" triggers a bottom sheet — not a full screen. Two options: Take a photo / Choose from gallery. Cancel at bottom. Sheet sits above dimmed content.
Native behaviour
On tap of either option, device's native camera or gallery opens. Hawksyn does not build a custom picker — it hands off to OS. Returns with selected image.

J5-S01c
Profile pic — crop preview, confirm or retake
Preview
Preview state
Photo selected. Shows circular crop preview — the exact shape it will appear as on the HIP. Two actions: "Use this photo" (amber primary) and "Choose again" (secondary).
Crop is fixed circle
No manual crop controls. The circle crop is auto-centred on the image. Simple and fast. If user wants different framing they tap "Choose again" and reselect.
