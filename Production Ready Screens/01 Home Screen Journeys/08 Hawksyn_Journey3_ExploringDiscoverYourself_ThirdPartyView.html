S23c
Recruiter Objections — what a recruiter will question before they meet you (flags only)
report_inputs.key_flags · flag_count=3 · visible only when flags exist
Conditional screen
This entire screen is hidden when flag_count = 0. Not an empty state. Not a clean profile message. The screen does not exist for zero-flag users. Only shown when key_flags[] has at least 1 entry. Salman has 3 flags → screen renders with 3 objection cards (max 3 shown — one per flag).
Objection card anatomy
Each card: 1 flag archetype_name (from key_flags[].archetype_name). Line 1 — "What they will ask" — pre-written question string per archetype_id (INTERVIEW_Q library). Line 2 — "Why this comes up" — evidence anchor plain label (Fix H) from psde_aeus[i].evidence_anchors[0]. Line 3 — "What to prepare" — pre-written 1-line prep note per archetype_id. All from pre-defined library. No LLM.
Data sources
key_flags[]: report_inputs, sorted by confidence_score descending. Max 3 cards. archetype_name from psde_aeus. Question string and prep note from INTERVIEW_Q text library (one entry per negative archetype). Evidence anchor: psde_aeus[i].evidence_anchors[0].anchor_type → plain label (Fix H). If archetype not in INTERVIEW_Q: fallback "Tell me more about {archetype_name} in your work."

"Some of your technical skills look dated. What have you been learning in the last 12 months?"
Why this comes up: Hawksyn detected legacy skill patterns in your profile against current market signals.
Prepare: Have a clear answer on which skills you have updated and what you are currently learning.

"Can you walk me through something you owned fully — not led as part of a team?"

Prepare: Identify 2 outcomes you owned end-to-end and can describe without saying "we."
