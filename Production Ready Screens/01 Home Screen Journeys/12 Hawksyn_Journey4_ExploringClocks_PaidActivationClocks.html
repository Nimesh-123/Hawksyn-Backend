S04-B

Processing
Payment sheet
Tapping "Activate my clocks" triggers the native Razorpay/payment sheet. Once user confirms payment → sheet closes → Hawksyn shows an inline processing state. Progress bar on the clocks screen, not a loading spinner.
What "processing" means
Payment confirmed → Hawksyn activates the paid plan server-side → clock engine starts the 180-day cycle → first refresh scheduled for 30 days from now. Processing bar is brief (~1.5s). Then transitions directly to the activation success screen.

S04-D · Monthly refresh notification — push + in-app banner
S04-D
Refresh notification — push alert + in-app banner on clock refresh day
Refresh day
Refresh day
On the 18th of each month (or whichever day they activated), Hawksyn runs the recalibration in the background. Push notification fires when scores are ready. User opens the app → green refresh banner at top → clocks show updated scores.
Critical drop alert variant
If any clock drops >15% in one month, the push notification changes tone: "⚠ Your AI Exposure just jumped. Check your clocks." Banner turns red. The score that dropped is highlighted with a red change indicator — ↓ from 61 to 34.
