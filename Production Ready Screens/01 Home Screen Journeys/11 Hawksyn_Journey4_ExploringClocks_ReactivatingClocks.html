S03 · Clocks Frozen — 3 states
STATE 1 — Frozen idle · All 4 clocks greyed · Banner at top
S03·1
Clocks frozen — expired, greyed out, growth prompt banner
Frozen
Visual grammar
All 4 clocks desaturate completely. Arc strokes go to #1a1a1a. Score shows "--". Card backgrounds flatten to near-black. No colour anywhere in the grid. Status bar goes from green live dot to grey frozen dot. The contrast with the live state is immediate.
Banner — right tone
Top amber banner: "6 months have passed. You have grown. Update your CV to restart." — not an error. Not an alert. A milestone prompt. Amber colour = action needed, but not urgency. Different from red = danger.
Tapping a grey clock
Tapping any greyed clock does NOT navigate to detail. Instead: a brief shake animation fires on the tapped card + a small inline tooltip appears below it: "Update your CV to see your scores again." Dismisses after 2s.

Last score memory
Each frozen card shows the last known score in dim text: "Last: 34 · Jun 2026". User can see what their scores were — but knows it is outdated. Ghost data, not current data. Motivates action without deleting context.
Nav icon change
Clocks bottom nav icon shifts from amber to dim grey when frozen. Active tab but inactive clocks — consistent signal. Tab is still selected but the system state is communicated through the icon colour change.
STATE 2 — Tap frozen clock · Card shakes · Inline tooltip appears
S03·2
Frozen clock tapped — shake micro moment + tooltip
Tap · frozen
Shake animation
Tapping a frozen clock triggers a 300ms horizontal shake (±4px, 3 oscillations). It is not a punishing animation — it is a "not available" signal. Like tapping a locked door. Then stops. Tooltip appears below the card.
Tooltip
Small dark card below the tapped clock: "Update your CV to see your scores again." Amber chevron right. Tapping the tooltip goes directly to the CV upload screen. Auto-dismisses after 2.5 seconds if not tapped.

Why shake not block
A modal or full-screen block would be aggressive. The shake says "not right now" without interrupting the user. They get one light signal, one tooltip, and they decide what to do. The banner at top handles the primary CTA — the shake just confirms the card is not active.
STATE 3 — Clocks restarting · CV uploaded · Clocks waking back up
S03·3
Restart — CV re-uploaded, clocks waking up, scores counting from 0
Restarting
Restart moment
User re-uploaded their updated CV. Hawksyn is now re-scanning. The clock grid transitions from the grey frozen state to the live entry animation (same as S01 State 1). Scores count up from 0. Each card lights up sequentially with its colour returning. The screen comes alive again.
Banner transforms
The amber "frozen" banner transforms into a green "Clocks restarting…" banner. Progress bar fills left to right. When complete it shows "Clocks updated. Welcome back." for 2 seconds then disappears — clocks take over.

Nav icon turns green
During restart, the Clocks nav icon turns green — not amber. Signals something positive is happening. Changes back to amber (active) once all 4 clocks are fully loaded. Three states: grey (frozen) → green (restarting) → amber (live).
Progress banner copy
"Re-scanning your updated CV · Hawksyn is reading the new you" — not "processing" or "loading." Language stays in the product's voice — serious, direct, slightly personal. Reminds the user why they just did this.
