M-PIN intro
Purpose
User tapped Begin on Card 3. This screen appears before the PIN entry. Sets clear expectations before asking for sensitive input.
Three things explained
1. Protects HIP — use case. 2. Hawksyn cannot see it — trust. 3. Yours alone — ownership. Each in a separate card. Plain language, no legal phrasing.
Build My HIP
Create your M-PIN
A 6-digit PIN that protects your intelligence profile.
Protects your HIP
Every time you open the app, you enter this PIN. Without it, no one can access your profile — not even on your device.
Hawksyn cannot see it
Your PIN is stored only on your device. We never receive it. If you forget it, you re-verify email and phone to reset.
Yours alone
Not biometrics. Not a password. Six digits you choose. Simple, private, always yours.

Mismatch state
