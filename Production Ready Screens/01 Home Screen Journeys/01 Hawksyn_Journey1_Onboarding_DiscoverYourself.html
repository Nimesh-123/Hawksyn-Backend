Why block
Consent is a legal requirement under DPDP. It must be an active opt-in — pre-checked boxes do not count. User actively ticks = valid consent recorded.
Design detail
Upload zone is passive (grey stroke, grey text) when consent is not given. Once ticked, zone brightens and button activates. Purely visual signal.
